Our Motive

Nasha Mukt Bharat Abhiyaan The Minister for Social Justice & Empowerment Shri Thawaarchand Gehlot launched the website for the Nasha Mukt Bharat Abhiyaan (NMBA) on the occasion of International Day Against Drug Abuse and Illicit Trafficking on 26 June 2021 which is observed worldwide to strengthen action and cooperation in achieving the goal of a sustainable world free of substance abuse. The entire world is facing the menace of drug addiction which has a devastating impact on the addict, individual, family.

GOVERNMENT SERVICES

By offering a wide array of services, including counseling, detoxification, rehabilitation, and aftercare, these centers play a pivotal role in helping individuals regain control over their lives and break free from addiction. Government-run Nasha Mukti Kendras are a testament to the state's dedication to creating a healthier and addiction-free society by providing accessible and effective solutions for those in need.
